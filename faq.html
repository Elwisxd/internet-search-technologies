Frequently Asked Questions

What are search engine algorithms?

Search engine algorithms are sets of rules and processes used to determine the relevance and ranking of pages in response to a user’s query.

How does the PageRank algorithm work?

PageRank evaluates the importance of a webpage based on the quantity and quality of links pointing to it. Pages with more high-quality backlinks rank higher in search results.

What is TF-IDF and how is it used?

TF-IDF (Term Frequency–Inverse Document Frequency) is a statistical method that determines the importance of a term in a document relative to a collection of documents. It improves content relevance matching.

How does artificial intelligence affect search engines?

AI technologies like Google RankBrain help search engines understand user intent, personalize results, and continuously learn to improve accuracy.

What is semantic search?

Semantic search aims to understand the meaning behind a query rather than just matching keywords. It uses NLP and context to provide more relevant results.

How does voice search impact SEO?

Voice search changes how queries are phrased—more conversational and longer. This affects SEO by encouraging natural language optimization and local targeting.

What are personalized search results?

Personalized search results are tailored based on a user's past behavior, location, and preferences to deliver more relevant outcomes.
